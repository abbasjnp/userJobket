About Jobket

A unique referral job platform that brings out quality in the hiring process!

Jobket is a platform that allows you to find suitable jobs for yourself and your family & friends. When you refer a good candidate, you not just increase chances of him/her finding a great opportunity but also win yourself rewards. Our only objective is 'Quality hiring'. So simply suggest who you think is the best fit for the job whether they're looking out or not. Callibre is key!
